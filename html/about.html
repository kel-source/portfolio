About Me

Aside from my deep obsession with front-end development, I am a friendly and enthusiastic year old from Brighton, England. I love to travel, drink coffee, play roller derby and learn new things.
After starting my coding journey in August during the first COVID lockdown of 2020, I have completed three of the four courses SheCodes provides to teach ultimate beginners.
