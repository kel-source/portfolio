< About Me />

Aside from my deep obsession with front-end development, I am a friendly and enthusiastic year old from Brighton, England. I love to travel, drink coffee, play roller derby and learn new things.
After starting my coding journey in August during the first COVID lockdown of 2020, I have completed three of the four courses SheCodes provides to teach ultimate beginners.

My Future

Coding and front-end development. It wasn't what I set out to do when I first began my degree in Forensic Science and it never once crossed my mind until I stumbled upon a live video introduction one day on social media. I had even decided to miss it. Needless to say, I didn't and a few subsequent courses later, I'm still hooked! I hope my future sees me designing and developing websites full-time, but for now, I look to continue developing professionally with courses in:

  • Web Design
  • Bioinformatics
